Team Sorter

Team sorter is a happy little website and free tool that allows to randomly compose two teams.
Below are the steps to use the tool.

How to use?
  1. Enter the name of the player in the input section below
  2. Press ADD button to enroll the chosed player's name to the list
  3. Repeat this process until the 10 players are added
  4. Press SORT button to randomly compose two teams
